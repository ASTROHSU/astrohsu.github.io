幣圈，究竟發生了什麼？What Happened to Crypto?

2025 年幣價再創新高、監管大幅鬆綁，但期待已久的「大場面」卻沒來。反而有人說「幣圈已死」，有人看到「工具萌芽」。In 2025, crypto hit new heights and regulation loosened. Yet the expected "big moment" never arrived. Some say "crypto is dead," while others see "tools sprouting."

PART 2界線正在消失BOUNDARIES ARE DISAPPEARING

過去幾年，穩定幣發行商一直依附於以太坊。但 2025 年情況改變了。Circle、Tether、Stripe 等重量級選手紛紛自建區塊鏈，不再仰賴任何公鏈。For years, stablecoin issuers depended on Ethereum. But in 2025, major players like Circle, Tether, and Stripe built their own blockchains, abandoning public chains.

他們的理由很簡單：穩定幣結算是核心業務，不能依賴無法控制的公鏈。Stripe 甚至花了 50 億美元打造支付專用鏈 Tempo，還挖角重量級以太坊研究員。Their logic is simple: stablecoin settlement is core business, and you can't rely on a chain you don't control. Stripe even spent $5B on Tempo, a payment-specific chain, recruiting top Ethereum researchers.

表面上看，大企業都來用區塊鏈了。但實際上，他們只需要區塊鏈的「技術」，不需要「幣圈」。這讓整個幣圈感到尷尬：應用層離去，公鏈能做什麼？On the surface, major companies adopted blockchain. But they only need the technology, not crypto. This leaves crypto communities uncomfortable: if applications leave, what's left for public chains?

PART 3尋找新土壤SEEKING NEW SOIL

a16z 合夥人 Chris Dixon 曾指出，每一個新技術的發展都分為兩個階段：a16z partner Chris Dixon pointed out that new technologies develop in two stages:

Chris Dixon 框架DIXON'S FRAMEWORK
每一個新的運算平台，都始於把舊事物做得更好，然後才開始賦予全新的事物。Every new computing platform starts by doing old things better, then enables entirely new things.
Email 最早是用來取代信件，圖的是「快速和免費」。誰能想到幾十年後，它催生出像區塊勢這樣的付費訂閱媒體？Email 不再只是省郵資，而是創造了全新的商業活動。Email started as a cheaper alternative to mail. Who could have imagined it would create subscription media like Blocktrend? Email created entirely new business models.

現在區塊鏈正處於第一階段——「把寄信變便宜」。它已經證明了價值，卻還沒找到能支撐長期發展的「新土壤」。Blockchain is now in stage one—making mail cheaper. It's proven its value, but hasn't found the "new soil" for sustainable growth.

追趕者Followers

努力讓區塊鏈做得更快、更便宜，想在大企業市場中爭一席之地。Try to make blockchain faster and cheaper, chasing enterprise market.

問題：Problem: 通用平台永遠打不過專用工具。到頭來，區塊鏈只會成為「比較慢的 Tempo」。General platforms never beat specialized tools. Blockchain becomes just "slower Tempo."

探索者Explorers

放棄追求企業市場，專注找出區塊鏈賦予全新事物的可能性。Stop chasing enterprise, focus on finding what blockchain enables entirely new.

問題：Problem: 新需求還沒出現。在等待過程中，代幣持有者的耐心會被磨光。New needs haven't appeared yet. Token holders' patience could run out while waiting.
2026 年的問題The 2026 Question
區塊鏈創業者如何在全新的市場環境裡，長出意想不到的新可能？不做交易、不發代幣，該怎麼賺錢？How can blockchain entrepreneurs discover new possibilities beyond trading and tokens?

「幣圈已死」和「工具萌芽」，其實說的是同一件事——界線正在消失。區塊鏈正在悄悄潛入日常，成為 Visa、Stripe、銀行的基礎建設。"Crypto is dead" and "tools sprouting" describe the same thing—boundaries are disappearing. Blockchain quietly embeds itself as infrastructure for Visa, Stripe, and banks.

但這一切都建立在一個前提上：有人願意在尚不可知的新市場中探索、投資、等待。就像當年相信 Linux 開放原始碼終有一天會成為必需品的那群人。But it all depends on someone willing to explore, invest, and wait in unknown new markets—like those who believed in Linux decades ago.

也正因如此，讀完了，你站在哪一邊就變得格外重要。That's why choosing a side after reading matters so much.
