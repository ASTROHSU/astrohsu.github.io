PART 1三個可能的未來Three Possible Futures

Vitalik 在貼文中明確指出，以太坊面臨三個選擇。每個選擇都有截然不同的結果，就像分岔路口一樣。三套劇本的核心差異，在於如何看待「去中心化」和「效能」的平衡。 Vitalik explicitly outlined three choices for Ethereum. Each has dramatically different outcomes, like a fork in the road. The core difference among the three scripts lies in how to balance "decentralization" and "performance."

PART 2為什麼 L2 不再是救世主Why L2 is no longer the savior

五年前，以太坊選擇把「效能」外包給 L2，主網專注「安全」和「去中心化」。這是一個很務實的妥協。但五年後的現在，情況改變了。 Five years ago, Ethereum decided to outsource "performance" to L2, leaving the mainnet to focus on "security" and "decentralization." A practical compromise. But things have changed five years later.

根據 Vitalik 的說法，有兩項關鍵原因： According to Vitalik, there are two key reasons:

第一，L1 擴容進展比想像更快。First, L1 scaling has progressed faster than expected. 零知識證明、Danksharding 等技術成熟度遠超預期。以太坊主網的 gas 上限預計大幅提升。如果主網的手續費最終夠便宜，使用者自然會直接用主網，根本不需要 L2。 Zero-knowledge proofs and Danksharding matured faster than expected. Ethereum's gas limit is set to increase significantly. If mainnet fees become cheap enough, users will naturally use it directly, with no need for L2.

第二，L2 去中心化進展比預期更慢。Second, L2 decentralization has progressed slower than expected. 一些 L2 團隊為了滿足客戶的監管需求，刻意保有對鏈的最終控制權。既然不打算走向完全去中心化，那就不算是在「擴展以太坊」，而是在做一條獨立的 L1。 Some L2 teams deliberately retained final control over the chain to meet customer regulatory requirements. If they're not planning true decentralization, they're not "extending Ethereum"—they're building an independent L1.

Vitalik 有點生氣：這些 L2 掛羊頭賣狗肉，自己卻混淆了身分。 Vitalik is frustrated: These L2s are mislabeling themselves while confusing their own identity.

賭注：Bet: 最悲觀的情景。但並非不可能。加密產業講究「贏者全拿」，一旦失去應用層，很難翻身。 The most bearish scenario. But not impossible. Crypto values "winner takes all." Once you lose applications, recovery is hard.

每個劇本都賭上了「時間」和「信仰」。但持有者的耐心是有限的。 Every script bets on "time" and "faith." But holder patience is finite.
