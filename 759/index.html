故事PART 1夜市的 100 股台積電100 TSMC Shares at the Night Market

今年過年，一名高中生在夜市套圈圈贏得大獎：100 股台積電。台積電股價逼近 2,000 元，換算下來這個獎品市值接近 20 萬元。This Lunar New Year, a high school student won the ring toss game at a night market. The prize: 100 shares of TSMC, worth nearly NT$200,000 at ~NT$2,000 per share.

但這也帶來一個實際問題：「夜市套圈圈贏的股票，要怎麼過戶？」But it raised a practical question: "How do you transfer stock won at a night market game?"

多數人不知道的是，股票可以轉贈，但流程繁瑣。攤位老闆必須向稅務機關申請完稅證明（3-5 個工作天），高中生必須在父母陪同下到券商辦理過戶申請，券商再將文件送交集保，整個流程至少一週，每個步驟還有手續費。Few know that stocks can be transferred, but it's cumbersome. The booth operator must request tax clearance (3-5 business days), the student must visit a broker with parents, documents go to the central custodian, and the whole process takes at least a week with multiple fees.

問題不在技術，而在於流程本身被設計得太複雜。The problem isn't technology—it's the process itself being unnecessarily complex.

為什麼要這麼複雜？因為股票轉讓需要更新股東名冊，確保那位高中生也能在股東會參與表決、領股息。但他真的在乎嗎？100 股對公司治理毫無影響。他只關心那 20 萬元的價值。Why so complex? Because stock transfers require updating shareholder records, ensuring voting rights and dividends. But does he care? 100 shares have no real say in governance. He only cares about the NT$200,000 value.

創新PART 2股票代幣如何改變一切How Tokenized Stocks Change Everything

美國公司 Ondo Finance 發行的股票代幣，採用「即時鑄造與贖回」機制。當有人想買美股代幣時，Ondo 在傳統券商同步下單，並在鏈上鑄造代幣。反之亦然。這樣就能確保價格永遠與市場同步。Ondo Finance tokenizes US stocks using "real-time minting and redemption." When someone buys a stock token, Ondo simultaneously orders from brokers and mints the token on-chain. This keeps prices perfectly aligned.

最近幣安上線了 Ondo 的美股代幣，包括蘋果（AAPLon）、谷歌（GOOGLon）、特斯拉（TSLAon）、輝達（NVDAon）等。用戶可以直接用美元穩定幣（USDC/USDT）買入，幾秒內完成鏈上轉移，手續費少到可以忽略。Binance recently launched Ondo's stock tokens including Apple (AAPLon), Google (GOOGLon), Tesla (TSLAon), and NVIDIA (NVDAon). Users can buy directly with stablecoins (USDC/USDT), transfer on-chain in seconds, with negligible fees.

那對夜市攤商而言意味著什麼？假設下次改用區塊鏈，攤商只需掃 QR code 轉帳，一瞬間就把台積電代幣送給那位高中生。不用稅局、不用券商、不用律師。What does this mean for the booth operator? Instead of using tax authorities, brokers, and lawyers, they can simply scan a QR code and send TSMC tokens on-chain in an instant.

效率差別不只是時間。它改變了人們使用股票的方式——從「嚴肅的投資工具」變成「日常流動性資產」。The difference isn't just speed. It transforms how people use stocks—from "serious investment tools" to "everyday liquid assets."

關鍵問題：你投資股票，是為了參與公司治理？還是單純追求價值增長？Key question: Do you invest for governance participation, or just for value appreciation?

股票代幣最有趣的地方，是它打開了一扇新的大門——不再是「只能去券商買股票」，而是「股票可以在日常生活中流轉」。The most fascinating thing about tokenized stocks is opening a new door: stocks are no longer confined to brokers but can flow through everyday life.

夜市攤商可以用它當行銷獎品，朋友間可以轉帳贈送，甚至可以在去中心化交易所交易。這不是傳統股票的簡單複製，而是一種全新的資產形式。Booth operators can use it as a prize, friends can gift it to each other, and it can be traded on decentralized exchanges. This isn't copying traditional stocks—it's creating an entirely new asset form.
